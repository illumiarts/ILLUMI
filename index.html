Addressable RGB LED technology for detailed, dynamic effects. Control every pixel for stunning visual patterns that react in real time.
Swappable Art Panels
Mirrored stainless steel anime art panels swap in seconds. Update your look to match your mood, season, or setup without any tools.
Sound-Reactive Modes
18 built-in music modes sync to your audio in real time. Pulses with your games, your stream, your music — automatically.
Bluetooth App Control
Mix colors, program 142 effects from your iOS or Android device. Alexa and Google Home compatible for voice control.
Premium Build
Anodized aluminum frame with mirrored stainless-steel surfaces. Not plastic. Museum-quality construction built to last.

Who We Are

About IllumiArts

Committed to Innovation & Driven by Light

The Company

IllumiArts LLC is a Washington DC-based lighting innovation company dedicated to redefining how we experience light through the seamless fusion of art, technology, and design. As pioneers in sound-reactive LED wall panels, we bring bold innovation to gaming rooms, modern homes, luxury hotels, and commercial spaces.

Our Expertise

Our award winning team of architects, designers, and engineers brings over 25 years of experience in architectural lighting and luxury commercial projects. We blend deep industry knowledge with creative vision to deliver premium, patented solutions. Every product is the result of years of prototyping, testing, and refinement.

How It’s Built

IA Smart panels are precision-manufactured at our long-standing partner facility in Shenzhen, China — a state-of-the-art factory we have worked with from early prototyping to full production. We closely oversee every stage for optimum quality. All products ship directly to your door via DHL Air Freight.
